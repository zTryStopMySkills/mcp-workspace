# Bonnet Cocktail Bar — Comprehensive Research Report
*Compiled: March 18, 2026*

## 1. OVERVIEW

**Bonnet Cocktail Bar** is a Spanish cocktail bar / shisha lounge with two locations in the greater Seville (Sevilla) area. It operates as a popular local gathering spot known for cocktails, hookahs (cachimbas), waffles, empanadas, live music, and DJ sets in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

## 9. ATMOSPHERE & UNIQUE SELLING POINTS

Based on aggregated customer reviews and platform descriptions:

- **"Bar de copas" (cocktail/nightlife bar)** — the core identity; not a restaurant
- **Hookah / shisha experience** is a primary draw and differentiator; expertly prepared by staff (Manu at Mairena praised specifically)
- **Waffles and empanadas** distinguish it from a pure cocktail bar — casual food offered
- **Staff as family** — multiple reviews use family/friendship language: "like family," "feel at home," "they mean a lot to me"
- **Named staff praised:** Gonzalo (Sevilla), Manu (Mairena) — personal service is a key differentiator
- **Music and events:** live concerts and DJ sets on certain nights
- **Clean bathrooms** — notably and repeatedly praised
- **Quality brands** — premium spirits highlighted
- **Terrace / outdoor seating** — both locations feature outdoor areas
- **Affordable:** perceived as good value for money
- **Popular starting point for an evening out** — described as a meeting place to "start the night"
- **Football-watching:** one review mentions watching football while enjoying hookah
- **Neighborhood bar feel** with a loyal local clientele
